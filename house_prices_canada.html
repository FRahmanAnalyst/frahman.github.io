HOUSE PRICE INCREASE
ACROSS CANADA (1985-2021)

Statistics Canada new house price index data, cleaned in MS Excel and
visualized in Tableau, to show increase in house prices in major
Canadian cities using 2016 as base price year.

The data used for this analysis was obtained from the New housing price index (monthly) table from Statistics Canada. The base year for price index in this table is December 2016. Microsoft Excel was used to clean the data from the table, removing the columns not required, renaming the columns, separating the locations into city, province and country and changing the date format.

Download Excel File (cleaned)

The cleaned data was then imported into Tableau Public to create the visualization below. It shows the timeline of price increase of new houses across the major Canadian cities from January 1985 until June 2021. It shows which cities have the highest average prices for new houses at a certain time as well as the price increase for a certain time period. The date sliders on each sheet and the dashboard can be adjusted for the time period required.
